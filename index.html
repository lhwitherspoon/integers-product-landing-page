Introducing The Integers

Tired of pairing up cats to water bowls to see if you are missing a cat?

Need a better way to keep track of orders received than moving a stone from one jar to the other?

Don't have enough fingers to match pizzas to friends?

Benefits of Using Integers

Multi-Use

Cats, cash, customers, pizza, shoes... the list goes on. You can use integers to count all of them. You name it, you can count it with integers.

Portable

What do you do if you are missing a cat and you aren't near a water bowl? Or get asked a question about orders and you aren't near the stones? Integers can be easily marked down on paper and carried with you.

Extendable

Unlike fingers, integers can be easily scaled up, all the way up to the Infinite Set©.

Integer Sets© do not include decimals or fractions.

Starter Integer Products*

One

Minimalist, perfect for small counting tasks. One sun.

Two

For when you you realize that everything has two sides. Two shoes.
